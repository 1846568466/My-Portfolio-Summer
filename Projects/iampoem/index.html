I Am Poem

I like pineapples
I wonder if there is a pineapple day
I hear the sound of pineapples laughing
I see a huge pineapple tree
I want infinite pineapples
I like pineapples

I pretend to be a pineapple
I feel the presence of pineapples around me
I touch the pineapples
I worry about the pineapples
I cry when they get eaten
I like pineapples

I understand that they are supposed to be eaten
I say that pineapples are the best
I dream of a land where pineapples can live freely
I try to fight for their rights
I hope that someday, they will achieve freedom
I like pineapples
